What's an unconference?

If this is your first unconference, congratulations! An unconference is a conference where sessions are programmed and led by the attendees. Unconferences are popular in the tech world because they reflect the culture of the industry â€“ hands-on, flexible, a little casual but very hard working. All the sessions are open for attendees to define, describe, and lead. You'll find the format to be energizing and entertaining.
